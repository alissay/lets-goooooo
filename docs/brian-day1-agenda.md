# Brian Day 1 - How We Work Now

## The honest version

AI has fundamentally changed what our jobs look like. This isn't about
prompting tricks. It's about a different mental model for where creativity
and craft actually live now.

## The 3 things that matter most

1. AI is the first drafter, never the final voice
   We use Claude/GPT to generate options fast. Our job is direction,
   judgment, and refinement - not generation. If you're staring at a
   blank canvas, you're doing it wrong.

2. Prompting is a design skill
   The quality of what you get out is directly related to how well you
   describe what you want. Specificity, constraints, examples, tone -
   this is craft now. We'll develop this together.

3. Speed creates permission to be bold
   Because we can generate 10 options in the time it used to take to make 1,
   we can afford to explore weirder territory. Say the crazy idea. We'll
   test it in 5 minutes.
